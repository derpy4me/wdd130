Site Name

Raft Wars

Goals

This website will increase outreach to potential customers and provide information to gain the interest of the customer.

This website will make it convenient for customers to see locations and times allowing them to plan on times to raft

By showing people having fun, we show that rafting is fun making the customer want to raft for fun

Websites are the way of the future and everybody uses google now to search for their interests.

Audience

We will most likely have the website be visited on mobile devices with families planning for family trips.

We will state that there is an age limit on the website

Costs for individuals and family bundles will need to be shown.

We are aiming for family fun

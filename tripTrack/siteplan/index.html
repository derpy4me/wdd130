Trip Track Siteplan

Elevator Pitch

When do you need to change your transmission fluid? How much time is left on your warranty?

Unless you have perfect memory or are obsessed with your car, you probably don't know these things off the top of your head. Bring in Trip Track, the vehicle expense and maintenance tracker. WIth this web app you will have all the information you need at the tip of your fingers. License plate number? Check! Last replaced spark plugs? 2 years ago! You can forget your worries about your vehicle and focus on more important things while being assured your vehicle is taken care of.

Site Purpose

The purpose of Trip Track is to help individuals keep track of expenses related to vehicles.

While helping with expenses, reminders a various vehicle needs will also be provided. Warranties, oil changes, and tire rotations to name a few.

The other purpose for trip track is to help those with similar vehicles help others with similar problems. Gathering different expeses and compiling into useful information can help customers in purchasing and maintaining different vehicles.

Target Audience

This site is targeted for individuals looking to keep track of vehicle expenses and wish to keep their car properly maintained.

The skill of the individuals could range from expert mechanics to those who simply own any type of vehicle.

A general vehicle consumer who wishes for one location to learn what it takes to maintain a vehicle and the costs incured.

Content

Expenses

Trip Track is for managing of vehicle expenses to help ensure that customers are aware of the vehicle costs. This can be helpful with the ever fluctuating costs of gas and oil. Also, can help to save for future maintenance giving a helpful estimate from previous checkups.


Maintenance

A vehicle can go further and provide a smoother experience when it is properly maintained. Most people don't know, or might not even care what mainteance of vehilce requires, prefering to drop it off at a mechanic demanding a fix. This can incur higher costs. Whether done yourself or by someone else it's easier to have little checkups and fixes at steady intervals than spending a large chunk of money when something breaks.


Remind

A vehicle has many small and large moving parts. All of these require checkups, and sometimes need to be replaced after a while. It can be difficult without being familiar with a vehicle to keep track of the bells and whistles involved. Reminding users that their vehicle should have a once over can help to reduce the chance of a large pile of cash disappearing into a mechanics pocket in the future.
